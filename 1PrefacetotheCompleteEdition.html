Preface to the Complete Edition

[TECHNICAL NOTE: Longer Writings, like this Preface, which might extend beyond one screenful of text, will appear in multiple columns, as in an ancient scroll, and may be read by moving the text itself left or right. However, for the sake of readability, lists and other apparati are displayed in a single column that can be shifted up and down.]

We Descend is the title given by present curator Bill Bly to his hypertext rendering of the Archives Pertaining to Egderus Scriptor, writings gathered and transmitted (by Egderus and others) over a span of many generations. Here collected in one edition will be found all the texts previously issued in three separate volumes.

Volume One of We Descend was published EV 1997 as a standalone computer application distributed by Eastgate Systems; Volume Two was brought out in the online journal The New River, Fall 2017. In addition, a 'preliminary view' of Volume Three appeared in The New River, Fall 2019. The edition you are reading now presents Volume Three in full, and includes a number of Writings heretofore unpublished.

Over the course of this project, the process of preparing the Writings has matured, with the inevitable consequence that some inconsistencies appeared not only in the narratives of certain Authors, but also in the apparatus in which they were housed. This omnibus edition attempts to smooth out that unevenness, though some irregularities were felt to be part of their story, and were retained.

Reading this hypertext

In any archive of writings, many voices speak, and it can take some time to discern how they are related to one another. In the present case, two entrances have been contrived, one for persons reading We Descend for the first time, and the other for return visitors. If this is your first time, the simplest way to embark on a journey into the Archives is to follow the PATHWAY, a kind of introductory tour, which departs from the word 'ENTER' on the TITLE page; this approach will take you through fifteen Writings of varying length. The PATHWAY will introduce you to most of the persons in We Descend and some of their stories.

At the bottom of each text field, there will appear a strip of text links leading BACKWARD and ONWARD, with a link in between them to PATHWAY, a list of all the Writings thereon.

At the PATHWAY's end, you will arrive at an interstitial directory called the LANDING, which will act as a portal to the many Writings you have not yet seen, and furnish other apparati that will prove useful in exploring the Archives further.
