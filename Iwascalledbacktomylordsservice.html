Scholion

I was called back to my lord's service

It is hard not to speculate here. It is surmised that there was some difficulty in the succession between the old Golias who sent (sentenced?) Robenc to Mountain House, and the new one who eventually appointed him Praetor. Was the Good Doctor implicated in this? was the Superius Frater?
