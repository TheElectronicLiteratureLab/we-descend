Legomenon for the Rectifier nicknamed the BONE-SNAPPER

The Author of {Steadfast Bone-Snapper} (possibly a dictation), can plausibly be identified with the Rectifier who did his deplorable work in the Office of (or for) Inquiry where Egderus first earned the cognomen Scriptor, apparently at the same time. The Writing itself tells us it was composed after he left office, but nothing is known of its transmission.
