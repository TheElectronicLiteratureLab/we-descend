SOLDIER

It is impossible not to imagine that the Writing {purple berries} is an adjunct, even a sequel, to the multiple narratives of the Exile, and indeed there is reason to suppose that we possessz the latter's work at all thanks to its rescue by this unnamed man-at-arms, who disobeys the orders of his superiors to follow his own sense of what is just.

Be that as it may, nothing in the Writings themselves explicitly links the two Authors other than (need it be said?) what we feel must be their relation to each other, despite coming upon these narratives in a state completely alienated from their original context, about which we know little if anything...
