Legomenon for Writings from the time of The ANCIENTS

The oldest writings in the Archives are survivors of the utter collapse of the magnificent civilization which flourished before our own. We first meet the Boy who, as a youth, accompanied the Ancient Author known as the Last One, whose own work gives us a hint of how we came into possession of the writings later found by Egderus and bound into a proper archive. These found texts by Unknown Ancients provide glimpses into the liminal period surrounding that apocalypse.
