Scholion

a potentially edifying pattern in which to read them

We do not know who (among the unknown number of unidentifiable scholars through whose hands the Archives have passed) performed the thoughtful labor of creating this edifying arrangement of the texts, and we may not agree that the purpose of such service has been fully achieved, but we must be grateful for their taking us into consideration in this interesting way.
