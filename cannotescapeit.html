Scholion

cannot escape it

The peculiar orthography in this passage provokes the suspicion that those phrases set off on a separate line and in a different size indicate the presence of migratory text, pre-existing the original inscription, that (in this case at least) may have been sufficiently well known as to require no citation. Unhappily that ur-text has never been retrieved except in this Writing, whose attribution cannot be ascertained.
