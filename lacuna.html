The Writing is fragmentary, and the break between these two sections may be a natural one, but the condition of the transcript is so degraded that this cannot be known for certain.

Likewise, it is impossible to identify the upheaval this horrendous scene represents — though one is tempted to assign it to the genocidal campaign the Golias carried out against the Remnant some years into Egderus' tenure as Superius Frater at Mountain House.
