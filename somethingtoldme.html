something told me

Another example of Egderus' practice of bringing up a question that he never answers. What difference could it possibly make whether Egderus gave the pages to Aric one at a time or all together? Unless Egderus is here merely invoking, in somewhat overdramatic terms, his customary prudence, we are left to wonder in vain.
