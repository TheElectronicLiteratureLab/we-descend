Scholion

that foolish boy

If a specific reference is intended here, it is unknown, but the scene is not hard to imagine, given the description: a comely young man, possessed by the yearning for love — or to love, as the case may be — lies daydreaming beside a slow-moving stream, and, rolling over, perceives a beautiful face floating just beneath the surface of the water, gazing at him with equal longing...
