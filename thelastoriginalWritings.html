Scholion

the last 'original' Writings

Some propose awarding the title Redactor to the supplier of the|Legomena ('sayings', 'what is or has been said about'; thus, 'elucidations'), since there is evidence of additional selection and shaping of the Writings at about this time — that is, some generations prior to their being rendered into hypertext form.
