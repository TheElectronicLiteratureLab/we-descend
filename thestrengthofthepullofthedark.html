the strength of the pull of the dark

Who can understand
the strength of the pull
of the dark?
It is not for understanding
but for fighting:
to make strong.
Forget the breaking until
it comes: everything breaks,
everything, all of it.
Instead, swarm with the drunken bees
upon the fallen peaches
rotting into the last lawn of summer,
heart high at all times.
